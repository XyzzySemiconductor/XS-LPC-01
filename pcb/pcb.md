### LPC Development board.

The LPC-DB PCB is a development board used to bring up and validate the LPC pump-controller ASIC by providing a controlled, 
low-voltage environment for CT sensing, signal conditioning, adc and digital interfacing during early hardware testing. 
It contains the CT burden input stage, protection components, calibration path, regulated supplies, and 
emulation header instrumenting the LPC chip IO. With the emulators wings clipped, 
the fits in the face of a Din rail module for field testing.

It does not switch mains and relies on external hardware for pump actuation.
